About Namma College

Tamil Nadu's most trusted free career guidance consultancy — helping students find their dream colleges since 2009.

Our Story

15+ Years of Free Guidance

Namma College was founded in 2009 in Coimbatore with one simple mission: provide every Tamil Nadu student access to quality career guidance — completely free of charge.

What started as a small consultancy has grown into Tamil Nadu's most trusted educational platform, helping thousands of students every year navigate college admissions, entrance exams, and career decisions.

Our team of experienced counsellors, educators, and career experts work tirelessly to ensure every student — regardless of their financial background — gets the guidance they deserve.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years


2009

Founded in Coimbatore

Namma College was established with a mission to provide free career guidance to Tamil Nadu students. Started with one-on-one counselling sessions in Coimbatore.

2015

Expanded Study Materials

Launched important question paper PDFs for Tamil Nadu State Board 12th exams. First year achieved 80%+ question match rate with actual board papers.

2019

Digital Expansion

Moved to digital platforms — WhatsApp, YouTube, and Instagram — reaching students across all districts of Tamil Nadu for the first time.

2021

Namma Global Careers Launched

Expanded internationally with Namma Global Careers, helping Indian healthcare professionals find opportunities in Germany.
